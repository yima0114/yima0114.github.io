About Me

Yi Ma is an artist trained in literature, oil painting, and jewelry design. She works across realist oil painting, printmaking, jewelry, wearable sculpture, and material experimentation. Painting provides the visual foundation of her practice, while close reading, observation, repetition, exhibition, and public response open new directions for later work.

Pipe Lady

Pipe Lady presents selected works from Yi Ma’s long-term pipe-focused practice, including the Fire Hydrant / Pipe Series, printmaking, and pipe-derived material and wearable forms.

Jewelry

Jewelry and material works explore how form, surface, weight, touch, and proximity change through relation to the body. The practice includes metal jewelry and wearable sculpture, alongside material experiments in paper, felt, foam, and acrylic.

CV

Education, artistic practice, exhibitions, publications, and professional experience across literature, oil painting, jewelry design, and material practice.
