Artist and researcher

Yi Ma

Artist-researcher working across painting, material translation, wearable forms, and public recognition.

My practice follows how forms and materials move between civic infrastructure, painted images, wearable objects, and collective memory. Through making and research, I consider how familiar public structures become visible, interpreted, and personally held.
