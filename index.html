About Me

Yi Ma is an artist working across painting, jewelry, wearable objects, and material transformation. Her practice explores how perception shifts through translation, from image to material, from surface to body, and from private attention to public encounter.

Pipe Lady

Pipe Lady begins with pipes and hydrants encountered in everyday urban space. Through painting, printmaking, and wearable forms, the project transforms overlooked forms into images and objects of attention, recognition, and memory.

Jewelry

Jewelry and material works extend Yi Ma’s painting-led practice into bodily scale and tactile encounter. Through metal, paper, felt, foam, and acrylic, images and forms are translated into objects that relate to body, touch, force, and scale.

CV

Education, exhibitions, selected outputs, and professional practice across literature, oil painting, jewelry design, material translation, and public encounter.
